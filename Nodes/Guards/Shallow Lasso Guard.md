# Overview
<u>Shallow Lasso Guard</u> is a variation of [Lasso Guard](obsidian://open?vault=Obsidian-BJJ-Notes&file=Guards%2FLasso%20Guard) where you frame with your foot on the shoulder instead of hook at the armpit.
# Entry
## Guards
- [Lasso](obsidian://open?vault=Obsidian-BJJ-Notes&file=Guards%2FLasso%20Guard): Switch between <u>Shallow Lasso</u> or regular [Lasso](obsidian://open?vault=Obsidian-BJJ-Notes&file=Guards%2FLasso%20Guard).
# Passing
- **Strip the frame**: Pin the guard player’s other leg to the mat with a pant grip or underhand knee grip, walk to the side of the <u>Shallow Lasso</u> until its gone.
## Passes
- [[Knee Cut]]: If the guard player makes it difficult to pass to the opposite side, duck under the <u>Shallow Lasso</u>.
- [[Leg Drag]]: As the they tries to square back up, step back and drag their other leg to your hip.
# Retention
- **Strip grips**: When the passer grabs your pants, strip their grips.
