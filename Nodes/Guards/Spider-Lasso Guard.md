# Overview
The <u>Spider-Lasso Guard</u> is a combination guard of Spider and Lasso Guard, where you hook one foot at the armpit and place the other foot on the bicep.
# Entry
- **Upper body control**: <u>Spider-Lasso Guard</u> is only accessible if the passer is leaning forward with their legs back.
## Guards
- [Lasso Guard](obsidian://open?vault=Obsidian-BJJ-Notes&file=Nodes%2FGuards%2FLasso%20Guard): Place your other foot on the bicep.
- [Spider Guard](obsidian://open?vault=Obsidian-BJJ-Notes&file=Guards%2FSpider%20Guard): Hook one foot at the armpit.
