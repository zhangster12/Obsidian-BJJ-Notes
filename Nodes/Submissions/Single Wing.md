# Overview
The <u>Single Wing</u> or Arm-in Lapel Choke or Katahajime is a lapel choke that involves using your opponent’s lapel and your other arm behind their head.

![Single Wing](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/3/3e/Kataha-jime_illustration.jpg/500px-Kataha-jime_illustration.jpg)
# Setup
- **Behind your opponent**: You need to be behind your opponent so you can pull their lapel and your elbow behind them.

# Keys
- **Remove the slack**: Grip the lapel at the collarbone level after removing all the slack; this makes the grip much tighter.
- Extend the arms
