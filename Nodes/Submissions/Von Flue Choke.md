# Overview
The <u>Von Flue Choke</u> is a choke that is used as a counter on the bottom player when they land on their backs while attempting a Guillotine.

![Von Flue Choke](https://cdn.evolve-mma.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/11/Narantungalag-Jadambaa-Von-Flue-Choke-ONE-1.jpg)
# Setup
- [Guillotine](obsidian://open?vault=Obsidian-BJJ-Notes&file=Submissions%2FGuillotine): While standing if your opponent falls to their back while finishing the submission.
# Keys
- **Shoulder pressure**: Drive your shoulder into the bottom player’s neck.

# Transitioning
- [[Side Control]]: If the bottom player lets go of their arms, you’ll be in a dominant position.
# Escape
- **Release the [Guillotine](obsidian://open?vault=Obsidian-BJJ-Notes&file=Submissions%2FGuillotine)**: You’ll be on the bottom, but you’ll have escaped the <u>Von Flue Choke</u>.
