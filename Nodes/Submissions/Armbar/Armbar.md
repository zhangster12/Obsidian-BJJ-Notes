# Overview
The <u>Armbar</u> is the hyperextension of an arm.

![Armbar](https://bjjfanatics.com/cdn/shop/articles/armbar1_1bfa0c0c-0032-410f-b3de-880cebf947e2_1024x1024.jpg?v=1564425862)
# Common Mechanics
- **Wrist and shoulder control**: Controlling both ends of the joint will prevent them from escaping and apply more breaking pressure. Control the wrist at the knuckles.
