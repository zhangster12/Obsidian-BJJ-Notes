# Overview
The <u>Front Body Lock Pass</u> is the main variation of the [[Body Lock Pass]] where you’re in front of the guard player.

![Body Lock Pass](https://www.bjjee.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/09/bodylock-pass-1.jpg)

# Defense
- **Knee-elbow connection**: Keep your knee-elbow connection will prevent the guard player from wrapping their arms around your waist.
## Guards
- [[Butterfly Guard]]: Frame on the neck (misaligns the passer’s head) and push their legs away as you sit up (prevents you from being flattened).
