# Overview
The <u>Body Lock Pass</u> is a pressure pass where you control the guard player's torso by wrapping your arms around their waist as you pass the guard.

![Body Lock Pass](https://www.bjjee.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/09/bodylock-pass-1.jpg)
