# Overview
The <u>Long Step</u> is a pass that disentangles you away from the legs of the guard player.

![Long Step](https://cdn.evolve-mma.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/10/long-step-pass-edited.jpg)
# Entry

# Keys
- **Grips**: ([1](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2FIBw6DJgAw&t=7s)) Grab a collar and pant grip in the gi or head and ankle for no-gi.
- **Shift back**: If you lean forward, you place your weight onto the guard player, making it easy for them to sweep you.
- 
- Drop the knee and elbow, then hips to the mat, while stepping the leg out.
- Then tripod.
## Troubleshooting
- **Passer comes up**: Stay on your toes, point your knee towards them, and drive your head into them after you complete the pass.
