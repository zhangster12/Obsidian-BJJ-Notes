# Setup
## [[Closed Guard]]
- Break their posture by dropping your hips and lifting your entire legs up
## Additional Details
- Use 2 on 1 grip
	- 1 hand at armpit, other at triceps
	- 1 hand at triceps other at wrist
- Bring your chest to their triceps
